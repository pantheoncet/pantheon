About Pantheon

Pantheon is the Annual Civil Engineering Technical fest of the College of Engineering Trivandrum, organized by the ICI CET Student Chapter. Over the years, Pantheon has evolved into one of Kerala's most anticipated student-led technical fests, blending innovation, competition, and collaboration on a grand stage.

From flagship events that challenge the brightest civil engineering minds, to hands-on workshops, exhibitions, and conclaves with industry leaders, Pantheon serves as a bridge between academia and industry. It brings together students, faculty, and professionals from across the state, fostering knowledge exchange, creativity, and professional growth.

With Pantheon 11 set to take place on September 26 & 27, 2025, this edition promises to raise the bar yet again - inspiring the engineers of tomorrow while celebrating a legacy of excellence, innovation, and teamwork.
